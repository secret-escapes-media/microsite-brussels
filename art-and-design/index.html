Parcours mode #16

The annual Mode Parcours or “Brussels Fashion Trail” has become a major event of interest both to the fashion sector and to the general public. Artistic installations in shop windows, art galleries, and a variety of (sometimes unusual) venues focus on creativity in Brussels. There are also workshops, open days, and other linked events.

The Power of Avant-garde

Avant-garde is a concept that stems from both warfare and art. Avant-garde flourished in a society in full transition. Artists anticipate social revolutions. In visual art the heyday of the avant-garde is situated between 1895 and 1920, with the First World War as an international fault line. But how relevant is this pioneering art today?

ADAM, Art & Design Atomium Museum

ADAM is a museum and art centre dedicated to the art and design of the 20th century and today. It offers a permanent exhibition called Plasticarium, which reveals a rich collection of plastic Golden Sixties objects and art works by Philippe Decelle, as well as art and design from the 20th and 21st centuries, all in a 5000m².
