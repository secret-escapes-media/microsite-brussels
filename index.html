Beat it to:

Brussels

The new and the Art Nouveau exist side by side in Belgium’s laissez-faire capital. From the magnificent Grand Place – one of the most beautiful squares in Europe – to the fresh crop of stylish hotels and restaurants, this must-visit city fascinates at every turn. Shop antiques in the fashionable Sablon area, discover homegrown Belgian designers amid the high-end boutiques and visit museums, bustling cafés and art exhibitions. If you’re there in October, don’t miss The Designers Trail at MODO Brussels – a three-day event celebrating the city’s creative talent with a programme of special events and parties.
